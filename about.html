Cactivism, a website focused on activism, was created by students Maya, Eve, Bella, Tanvi, and Bethelehem as their final project in Girls Who Code. Cactivism is a platform for activists around the world to share their opinions on world issues and to make a change.

If you have any questions or would just like more information about Cactivism and its founders, please contact us at cactivismco@gmail.com.
